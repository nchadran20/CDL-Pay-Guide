Getting Your CDL

How Much Does CDL School Cost in 2026?

The full cost breakdown — from school tuition to your license fees. Plus the most important thing most people don't know: companies will often pay for your CDL.

🏫

CDL School Tuition

$3,000 – $10,000

Private schools cost the most ($7K–$10K). Community colleges are cheaper ($3K–$5K) but have waitlists. Duration is typically 3–8 weeks.

📋

Permit & License Fees

$100 – $200

Commercial Learner's Permit ($10–$50), CDL license ($30–$100), and written/skills test fees vary by state.

🩺

DOT Physical + Drug Test

$100 – $200

Required before you can get your CDL. DOT physical ($50–$150), drug screen ($30–$60). Must be renewed every 2 years.

⚠️

Endorsement Fees (Optional)

$20 – $100 each

Hazmat, Tanker, Doubles/Triples each require additional tests. Hazmat also requires a TSA background check. But they boost your pay significantly.
