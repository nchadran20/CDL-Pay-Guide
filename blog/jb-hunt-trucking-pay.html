J.B. Hunt Trucking Pay and Reviews 2026: What Drivers Actually Earn

Updated: April 2026Read time: 8 min

J.B. Hunt is one of the largest and most respected trucking companies in the US — publicly traded, financially stable, and known for its intermodal operation that gives drivers better home time than traditional OTR. Here's the complete 2026 pay breakdown.

J.B. Hunt 2026 Pay Summary

Average driver earnings: $65,000–$90,000 depending on division. Intermodal division (best home time): $65,000–$80,000. Dedicated division: $70,000–$85,000. Sign-on bonus: up to $15,000 for experienced drivers.

J.B. Hunt's Intermodal Division — Why It's Different

J.B. Hunt pioneered intermodal trucking — moving containers between rail yards and destinations. Intermodal drivers handle shorter runs (typically 50–250 miles) and get significantly more home time than OTR drivers. The tradeoff is slightly lower per-mile rates compared to OTR, but many drivers find the quality of life improvement worth it.

J.B. Hunt has the largest intermodal operation in North America. If home time matters to you, JBI is one of the best options at a major carrier.

  • Medical, dental, vision insurance
  • 401(k) with 50% match up to 3% of salary
  • Quarterly performance bonuses
  • Paid time off
  • Employee stock purchase plan
  • Newer Peterbilt and International equipment
  • Safety bonus program

What J.B. Hunt Drivers Actually Say

J.B. Hunt consistently gets better reviews than most large carriers. Common positives: financial stability of a publicly traded company, newer and well-maintained equipment, strong intermodal division for home time, and consistent freight availability. Common complaints: dedicated accounts vary widely in quality depending on which account you're assigned, and some OTR drivers report inconsistent dispatcher communication.

J.B. Hunt is generally considered a step above the entry-level carriers like Swift and CR England in terms of pay and driver experience. Many drivers start at entry-level carriers and move to J.B. Hunt after gaining 1–2 years of experience.
