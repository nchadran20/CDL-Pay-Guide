Updated: May 2026Read time: 8 min

Here's exactly what CDL truck drivers earn in Los Angeles in 2026 — real salary data by experience level, driving type, and the top employers actively hiring in the area.

📊 Los Angeles Fast Facts for CDL Drivers

Average truck driver salary in Los Angeles: $69,000/year. Los Angeles is home to the busiest port complex in the US — the Ports of LA and Long Beach combined handle about 40% of all US container imports. Port drayage drivers in LA earn some of the highest local CDL wages in the country.

Top Employers Hiring in Los Angeles

  • Port of LA / Long Beach drayage carriers — Container drivers earn $75,000–$105,000 annually while going home nightly — the premium CDL opportunity in Los Angeles
  • Amazon — Massive LA fulfillment network with local delivery and linehaul positions throughout Southern California
  • UPS — Major LA operations with union Teamsters positions offering top pay and benefits
  • Sysco / US Foods — Food distribution throughout LA metro, local routes available
  • Ralphs / Kroger — Grocery distribution, local routes with consistent hours
  • XPO Logistics — Strong LTL presence in the LA market

How to Maximize Your Pay in Los Angeles

  • Port drayage is the best-paying local CDL work in LA — Container drivers at the port are home nightly and earn more than most OTR drivers nationally. Worth the effort to get into.
  • Union jobs pay significantly more — Teamsters positions in LA are widely available and pay 25–40% above non-union equivalents
  • AB5 affects owner-operators — If you're considering going independent in California, research AB5 carefully — it significantly restricts independent contractor trucking in the state
  • Agricultural reefer freight from the Central Valley — Refrigerated drivers hauling produce from Fresno and the San Joaquin Valley earn above-average rates
