Truck Driver Salary in Dallas, TX: (2026 Real Numbers)

Updated: May 2026Read time: 8 min

If you're a CDL driver in Dallas or considering a trucking career in the area, this guide gives you the real numbers. Here's exactly what truck drivers earn in Dallas in 2026 — by experience level, driving type, and which employers are paying the most.

📊 Dallas Fast Facts for CDL Drivers

Average truck driver salary in Dallas: $63,000/year. Dallas-Fort Worth is the largest metropolitan freight market in Texas. The DFW Airport generates massive air cargo ground freight, and the city sits at the crossroads of multiple major interstate freight corridors. No state income tax maximizes take-home pay.

Top Employers Hiring CDL Drivers in Dallas

  • Amazon — Multiple DFW fulfillment centers driving enormous local and linehaul demand
  • J.B. Hunt — Strong intermodal and dedicated presence in the DFW market
  • Werner Enterprises — Major DFW regional hub with OTR and regional routes
  • American Airlines Cargo — DFW Airport cargo ground operations with strong local pay
  • Walmart — Multiple DFW distribution centers with dedicated routes
  • McLane Company — Temple, TX-based wholesale distributor with strong DFW presence

How to Maximize Your Pay in Dallas

  • No state income tax is a significant advantage — DFW drivers keep more of their earnings than drivers in comparable metro markets in taxed states
  • DFW Airport cargo pays premium rates — Air freight ground operations command higher pay than standard freight in the DFW market
  • Target the 820 loop warehouse corridor — Fort Worth's industrial areas along I-35W and 820 have dense distribution center activity with strong driver demand
  • Oil field support from DFW — Some carriers run West Texas oil field freight from DFW — these routes pay significantly above standard DFW rates
