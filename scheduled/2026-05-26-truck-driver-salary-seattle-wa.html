Updated: May 2026Read time: 8 min

Here's exactly what CDL truck drivers earn in Seattle in 2026 — real salary data by experience level, driving type, and the top employers actively hiring in the area.

📊 Seattle Fast Facts for CDL Drivers

Average truck driver salary in Seattle: $74,000/year. Seattle is one of the top-paying CDL markets in the entire US. The Ports of Seattle and Tacoma create massive drayage demand, and Washington has no state income tax — meaning top-dollar wages with full take-home pay.

Top Employers Hiring in Seattle

  • Port of Seattle / Tacoma drayage carriers — Puget Sound container drivers earn $78,000–$105,000 annually with nightly home time — the premier local CDL work in the Northwest
  • Amazon — Amazon's home market — massive fulfillment network with strong pay and volume
  • Boeing Supply Chain — Aerospace freight and specialized logistics around the Puget Sound area
  • Albertsons / Safeway — Northwest grocery distribution, local routes available
  • COSCO Shipping — International freight operations through Seattle ports
  • UPS — Strong Seattle operations with union positions available

How to Maximize Your Pay in Seattle

  • No state income tax is Seattle's biggest advantage — Already among the highest CDL wages in the US, zero state tax means Seattle drivers keep significantly more than comparable earners in California or New York
  • Port drayage is elite pay with local lifestyle — Puget Sound port drivers earn $80,000–$105,000 going home every night — one of the best quality-of-life/income combinations in CDL driving anywhere in the country
  • Agricultural reefer freight pays premium rates — Washington produces enormous apple, hop, and produce crops requiring refrigerated transport with strong per-mile rates
  • Amazon Seattle operations pay above market — Being in Amazon's home city means access to better-compensated routes and more volume than most Amazon markets
Compare Seattle to Other Markets
