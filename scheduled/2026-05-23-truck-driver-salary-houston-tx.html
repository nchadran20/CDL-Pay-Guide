Truck Driver Salary in Houston, TX: (2026 Real Numbers)

Updated: May 2026Read time: 8 min

If you're a CDL driver in Houston or considering a trucking career in the area, this guide gives you the real numbers. Here's exactly what truck drivers earn in Houston in 2026 — by experience level, driving type, and which employers are paying the most.

📊 Houston Fast Facts for CDL Drivers

Average truck driver salary in Houston: $66,000/year. Houston is the highest-paying CDL market in Texas. The Port of Houston is the busiest port in the US by tonnage, and the oil and gas industry creates demand for specialized hazmat and tanker drivers at premium rates. No state income tax keeps all earnings in your pocket.

Top Employers Hiring CDL Drivers in Houston

  • Port of Houston drayage carriers — Container drivers at the Port of Houston earn $75,000–$100,000 annually and are among the highest-paid local CDL drivers in Texas
  • Halliburton / SLB — Oil field services companies in Houston pay top dollar for hazmat and tanker CDL drivers
  • Amazon — Multiple Houston-area fulfillment centers with strong local and linehaul demand
  • H-E-B — Texas grocery chain with one of the most respected private fleets in the state — excellent pay and benefits
  • Kinder Morgan — Pipeline and energy company with specialized CDL driver needs throughout the Houston energy corridor
  • US Foods / Sysco — Major food distribution operations across the Houston metro

How to Maximize Your Pay in Houston

  • Port drayage is the highest-paying local CDL work in Houston — Container drivers working the Port of Houston are home nightly and earn premium rates — the best combination in the Houston market
  • Get your Hazmat + Tanker (X) endorsement — Houston's oil and gas industry creates massive demand for X-endorsed drivers. Experienced hazmat tanker drivers in the energy corridor earn $85,000–$115,000 annually
  • Energy corridor positions pay the most — CDL jobs supporting the oil and gas industry in the Katy/Energy Corridor area consistently pay above standard freight rates
  • No state income tax maximizes your earnings — Houston's higher wages combined with zero state income tax makes it one of the most financially rewarding CDL markets in the US
