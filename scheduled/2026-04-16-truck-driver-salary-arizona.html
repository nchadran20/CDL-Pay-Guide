How Much Do Truck Drivers Make in Arizona? (2026 Salary Guide)

Updated: April 2026Read time: 8 min

If you're considering a CDL career in Arizona — or already driving and want to know if you're being paid fairly — this guide gives you the real 2026 numbers. We break down average truck driver salaries in Arizona by experience level, driving type, and city.

📊 Arizona Fast Facts for CDL Drivers

Average truck driver salary in Arizona: $54,800/year. Arizona has a flat state income tax rate of 2.5% — one of the lowest income tax rates in the country. Phoenix is a rapidly growing logistics hub. Arizona's location between California and Texas makes it a key freight corridor state. Year-round warm weather means no winter driving issues.

CDL Requirements in Arizona

To drive commercially in Arizona you need a valid Arizona CDL. Key requirements:

  • Must be 18+ for intrastate (within Arizona) driving
  • Must be 21+ for interstate (crossing state lines) driving — federal requirement in all states
  • Valid Arizona driver's license and DOT medical certificate
  • Pass written knowledge tests and a skills test
