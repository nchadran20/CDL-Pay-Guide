Truck Driver Salary in Nashville, TN: (2026 Real Numbers)

Updated: May 2026Read time: 8 min

If you're a CDL driver in Nashville or considering a trucking career in the area, this guide gives you the real numbers. Here's exactly what truck drivers earn in Nashville in 2026 — by experience level, driving type, and which employers are paying the most.

📊 Nashville Fast Facts for CDL Drivers

Average truck driver salary in Nashville: $57,000/year. Nashville is one of the fastest-growing cities in the US — and freight demand is growing with it. No state income tax means every dollar you earn stays in your pocket. Major distribution hub with Amazon, FedEx, and hundreds of distribution centers.

Top Employers Hiring CDL Drivers in Nashville

  • Amazon — Multiple fulfillment centers around Nashville driving massive local and linehaul demand
  • FedEx — Major Nashville operations across all service types
  • Dollar General — Headquarters in Goodlettsville (Nashville suburb), large private fleet with competitive pay
  • Averitt Express — Nashville-based regional carrier with strong reputation and solid pay
  • Sysco — Food distribution throughout Nashville, local routes with daily home time
  • Werner Enterprises — Strong OTR and regional presence through Nashville corridors

How to Maximize Your Pay in Nashville

  • No state income tax advantage — A $57,000 Nashville salary effectively equals $62,000–$65,000 in comparable high-tax states like California or New York
  • Target distribution centers on the outskirts — Lebanon, Smyrna, and Murfreesboro have massive warehouse corridors with strong local CDL demand and competitive pay
  • Amazon linehaul pays well — Nashville's central Amazon network creates strong linehaul opportunities between major hubs
  • Get your Hazmat endorsement — Opens doors to fuel transport and chemical freight throughout the Middle Tennessee market
