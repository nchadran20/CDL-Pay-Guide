📊 Miami Fast Facts for CDL Drivers

Average truck driver salary in Miami: $63,000/year. Miami is the highest-paying CDL market in Florida. Port of Miami and PortMiami handle enormous international freight volumes, and Miami's role as the gateway to Latin America creates unique freight opportunities. Florida has no state income tax.

How to Maximize Your Pay in Miami

  • PortMiami drayage is the best-paying local CDL work in South Florida — International container freight commands premium rates and drivers go home nightly
