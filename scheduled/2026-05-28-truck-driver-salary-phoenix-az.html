Truck Driver Salary in Phoenix, AZ: (2026 Real Numbers)

Updated: May 2026Read time: 8 min

Here's exactly what CDL truck drivers earn in Phoenix in 2026 — real salary data by experience level, driving type, and the top employers actively hiring in the area.

📊 Phoenix Fast Facts for CDL Drivers

Average truck driver salary in Phoenix: $61,000/year. Phoenix is one of the fastest-growing freight markets in the US. Companies fleeing California's regulations are relocating distribution operations to Phoenix, driving rapid driver demand growth. Arizona's 2.5% flat income tax is one of the lowest in the country.

Top Employers Hiring in Phoenix

  • Amazon — Multiple Phoenix-area fulfillment centers — one of the largest CDL employers in the city and growing rapidly
  • Swift Transportation — Headquartered in Phoenix, home-market operations mean strong driver support and route availability
  • Werner Enterprises — Strong Southwest regional routes through Phoenix
  • Frito-Lay — Large Phoenix distribution operation with local delivery routes
  • Walmart — Multiple Phoenix-area distribution centers
  • Shamrock Foods — Phoenix-based food service distributor with local routes throughout Arizona

How to Maximize Your Pay in Phoenix

  • Arizona's 2.5% flat income tax is one of the lowest in the US — Significantly more take-home than comparable California markets across the border
  • Phoenix freight growth is accelerating — Massive new distribution center development is creating strong upward pressure on driver wages throughout the metro
  • Swift Transportation home market advantage — Being in Swift's home city means better dispatcher relationships, newer equipment priority, and more route options than other markets
  • Year-round warm weather means no winter downtime — Phoenix drivers can run consistently throughout the year without weather-related slowdowns
