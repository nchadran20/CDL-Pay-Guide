CR England Pay Rate 2026: What Drivers Actually Earn

Updated: April 2026Read time: 8 min

CR England is one of the largest refrigerated freight carriers in the US and one of the most well-known company-paid CDL training programs available. Here's exactly what CR England pays in 2026 and what you should know before signing on.

CR England 2026 Pay Summary

Starting pay: $0.48–$0.56/mile. Average first-year earnings: $50,000–$62,000. Refrigerated specialist (experienced): $65,000–$78,000. CR England Truck Driving School pays 100% of tuition.

CR England Truck Driving School

CR England operates one of the most established CDL school programs in the country — CR England Truck Driving School. Details:

  • 100% tuition paid by CR England
  • 1-year commitment to drive for CR England after graduation
  • Training available at multiple locations nationwide
  • Refrigerated freight specialty training included
  • Graduates enter CR England's driving program with a guaranteed job

The Refrigerated Advantage

CR England specializes in refrigerated freight — transporting temperature-controlled goods like produce, dairy, meat, and pharmaceuticals. Reefer driving typically pays $0.02–$0.05/mile more than dry van because of the added responsibility and temperature monitoring requirements. As you gain experience at CR England, transitioning to premium refrigerated accounts pays significantly more than standard dry van OTR.

What CR England Drivers Say

CR England reviews are mixed. Positives: good training program, consistent refrigerated freight, and a clear path from school grad to experienced reefer driver. Negatives: starting pay is on the lower end of major carriers, lease purchase programs offered by CR England are widely criticized by experienced drivers — avoid lease-purchase and drive company equipment instead.
